Who am I?

Hello, My name is Michael Meding

or just Mike for short. I'm a Computer Science major at UMass Lowell and enjoy many disciplines of programming including web-coding.

Hobbies?

In my spare time I am an avid cyclist often riding hundreds of miles a week. If I'm not in front of my PC I'm lost somewhere out on a ride.

In addition to cycling, I also play around with an firmware based coding such as the arduino.

Little arduino project I did

How did you get started?

I really got interested in programming when I started working at this company called Outsmart. When I started I was working on the product assembly line churning out little plastic devices all day. Luckily, as production started to slow down I was moved to writing small scripts to automate some of the manufacturing tests/processes. From there it was a downward spiral until I was drinking 5 cups of coffee a day and churning out thousands upon thousands of lines of enterprise code.
